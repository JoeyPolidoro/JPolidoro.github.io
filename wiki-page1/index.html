For the album by Trash Boat, see Crown Shyness (album).

Crown shyness (also canopy disengagement.[1] canopy shyness,[2] or inter-crown spacing[3])This is most prevalent among trees of the same species, but also occurs between trees of different species.[6][7] There exist many hypotheses as to why crown shyness is an adaptive behavior, and research suggests that it might inhibit spread of leaf-eating insect larvae.[8]
